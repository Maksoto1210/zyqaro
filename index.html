    FAQ

    Questions people ask before choosing a name

    The generator is designed to help with early exploration, shortlisting, and faster decision-making.

    How does the name generator work?

    It combines structured word banks, category-specific naming patterns, style rules, keyword blending, and duplicate filtering to create brandable suggestions that feel more intentional than simple random output.

    Can I use these names for my business?

    Yes, you can use the suggestions as inspiration or as a starting shortlist. Before committing, you should still check trademarks, domain availability, and social handle availability in your market.

    Are the generated names unique?

    The tool filters duplicates within each result set, but it does not verify legal or market uniqueness. Treat each suggestion as a naming direction to research before launch.

    How do I choose a strong brand name?

    Look for names that are easy to pronounce, easy to spell, distinctive in your category, and still meaningful after a few hours away from the screen. Saying the name out loud is one of the fastest quality checks.

    What should I do after finding a name I like?

    Save it, copy it into your working notes, then check domains, search results, trademarks, and social handles. It also helps to see the name in context, such as a homepage title, app icon, or simple wordmark.

    Can I generate usernames too?

    Yes. Switch the category to Username and the tool will favor compact handle-style outputs that are easier to use across social platforms, creator pages, and personal branding.
